Sponsors

Symfony CAMP UA Conference is dedicated to web applications development using PHP Framework Symfony. The primary conference theme is the Symfony 2 and Symfony 3 frameworks.

The conference is targeted at both professional web-developers and beginners, as well as heads of Internet projects, web studios which are already using or are only planning to use Symfony framework. The main aim of the conference consists in exchange of experience, communication and new contacts.

We’re looking for sponsors and partners who could support the conference. This would help us to hold the conference at a high level and make participation possible for the wide audience.
